Getting Started Follow these steps to start playing your first game as quickly as possible:

Sign Up

When you sign up you create an ID for yourself that you'll need to use when you login to PredictWeasel. You'll receive an email shortly afterwards confirming your details.

Play!

At this point you'd either create your own league or wait from an invitation from another player to join a league they've created.

However, as it's not yet possible for players to create their own leagues (because I've not had time to write that bit before the World Cup starts), I'll automatically subscribe you to the only league in town as soon as you've signed up.

You can now play the game. Enter your predictions, wait for the results, and see where you stand in the league.

Have fun!
