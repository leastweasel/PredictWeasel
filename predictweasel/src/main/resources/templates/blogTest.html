Launch Day Looms

  • Fri 6th June 2014

Excitement as the launch of the new-look PredictWeasel draws close.

Tuesday June 10th will see the launch of PredictWeasel 2.0, in time for the 2014 FIFA World Cup. Version 2.0 is a much needed upgrade to the venerable prediction game that first appeared in 2004.

The decision to rewrite PredictWeasel in time for the World Cup was only taken a week or so ago so it has been something of a race against time to get it finished. There's still some functionality missing but it's sufficient to play the game.

The first match of the World Cup will be played on Thursday 12th June.
