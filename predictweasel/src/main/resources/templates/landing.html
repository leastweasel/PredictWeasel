Welcome to PredictWeasel

PredictWeasel hosts score prediction games. You and the other players predict the results of sports fixtures and, the better your prediction, the more points you score. At the end of the competition, the players with the most points win the prizes.

The most recent leagues were based on the 2010 FIFA World Cup. The next is just about to start and will be based on the 2014 FIFA World Cup, which starts in Brazil on Thursday 12th June, and continue until Sunday 13th July.
