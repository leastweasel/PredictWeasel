Notes

Please fill in the form to register as a user of PredictWeasel. Until you've registered, you won't be able to make predictions or join any leagues.

You'll be able to change your details later if you wish.

Your Email Address is what you'll use to log in to PredictWeasel. It must be unique and you will be told if someone has already registered with that address. This is also the address PredictWeasel will use to notify you of anything important.

You'll also have to supply a Password when you log in. Enter the same value for the Password and Confirm Password fields. In case you forget your password you can also enter a Password Reminder. If you later need to reset your password, because you've forgotten it, we'll email you this reminder.

The Name is how you'll appear as to others on the web site, so bear that in mind!
